Simulation

The following simulation shows a dynamic segment always tangent to the curve $y=f(x)$ at $x=a$. The slope of this tangent segment is equal to $f'(a)$.

Things to try:

  • Slowly drag the point to observe how the slope of the tangent segment changes.
  • Active the box Trace derivative to sketch the derivative.
  • Calculate the derivative and enter your answer to check the result.
  • Change the function f(x). Examples:
    • (x-2)/(x^2-4x)
    • exp(-x^2-1)
    • sqrt(x^2+2)
    • 1/2sin(x-1)^2
    • arctan(x/(x+1))
